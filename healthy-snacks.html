Greek Yogurt Berry Parfait

Prep Time: 5 minutes
Calories: 250 kcal
Protein: 15g
Category: High Protein

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Greek yogurt (full-fat or 2%)
  • 1/2 cup fresh mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • 1/4 cup homemade granola or store-bought
  • 1 tablespoon raw honey
  • Optional: 1 teaspoon chia seeds
  • Optional: Fresh mint leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a serving glass or mason jar, start with a layer of Greek yogurt (about 1/3 of the yogurt).
  2. Add a layer of mixed berries (about 1/3 of the berries).
  3. Sprinkle a layer of granola (about 1/3 of the granola).
  4. Repeat the layers twice more, ending with granola on top.
  5. Drizzle with honey and sprinkle chia seeds if using.
  6. Garnish with fresh mint if desired and serve immediately.

Almond Date Energy Balls

Prep Time: 15 minutes
Calories: 120 kcal
Category: Energy Boost

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dates, pitted
  • 1 cup raw almonds
  • 1/4 cup dark chocolate chips
  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil
  • Optional: 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Optional: Pinch of sea salt

Instructions

  1. Place almonds in a food processor and pulse until finely chopped.
  2. Add dates and process until well combined and mixture starts to stick together.
  3. Add chocolate chips and coconut oil, pulse briefly to combine.
  4. Roll mixture into 1-inch balls using your hands.
  5. Place on a lined baking sheet and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
  6. Store in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 2 weeks.

Spiced Roasted Chickpeas

Prep Time: 35 minutes
Calories: 180 kcal
Category: Crunchy

Ingredients

  • 1 can chickpeas
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Rinse and dry chickpeas
  2. Mix with oil and spices
  3. Roast in oven
  4. Season with salt and pepper
