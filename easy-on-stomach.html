Banana Oatmeal

Prep Time: 10 minutes
Calories: 310 kcal
Servings: 1
Category: Easy to Digest

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1 ripe banana, mashed
  • 2 cups water
  • Pinch of salt
  • Honey (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a pot, bring water to a boil.
  2. Add rolled oats and reduce heat to medium-low.
  3. Cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Mash the banana and add it to the oatmeal.
  5. Cook for another 2-3 minutes until desired consistency.
  6. Add a pinch of salt and honey if desired.
310 Calories
8g Protein
62g Carbs
8g Fiber

Rice and Steamed Vegetables

Prep Time: 20 minutes
Calories: 220 kcal
Servings: 2
Category: Light Meal

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white rice
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Rinse rice until water runs clear.
  2. Cook rice with 2 cups of water.
  3. While rice cooks, prepare vegetables.
  4. Steam vegetables for 8-10 minutes until tender-crisp.
  5. Season with a small amount of salt.
  6. Serve vegetables over rice.
220 Calories
5g Protein
45g Carbs
4g Fiber

Homemade Applesauce

Prep Time: 25 minutes
Calories: 95 kcal
Servings: 4
Category: Snack

Ingredients

  • 4 apples, peeled and chopped
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 tsp cinnamon (optional)
  • 1 tbsp honey (optional)

Instructions

  1. Peel and chop apples into chunks.
  2. Place apples and water in a pot.
  3. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat.
  4. Simmer for 15-20 minutes until soft.
  5. Mash or blend to desired consistency.
  6. Add cinnamon and honey if desired.
95 Calories
0.5g Protein
25g Carbs
4g Fiber

Simple Avocado Toast

Prep Time: 5 minutes
Calories: 290 kcal
Servings: 1
Category: Light Meal

Ingredients

  • 2 slices whole grain bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • Salt to taste
  • Lemon juice (optional)

Instructions

  1. Toast the bread slices.
  2. Cut avocado in half and remove pit.
  3. Mash avocado in a bowl.
  4. Spread mashed avocado on toast.
  5. Add a pinch of salt.
  6. Squeeze lemon juice if desired.
290 Calories
8g Protein
33g Carbs
12g Fiber
